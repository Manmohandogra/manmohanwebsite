About

Manmohan Dogra is a distinguished tabla virtuoso from the Banaras Gharana, with extensive knowledge of Delhi and Ajrada styles. With over 20 years of experience in both solo and accompaniment performances, he has dedicated his life to mastering the ancient art of Indian classical percussion.

His musical journey began under the guidance of Pt. Vijay Shankar Mishra, and he holds a Bachelor and Master's in Music (Percussion) from the University of Delhi, as well as an MSc in Music Business from Edinburgh Napier University.

Playing the tabla is not just about mastering compositions but about developing a true sense of rhythm that can travel across traditions. Fusion is not about replacing one form with another; it is about blending them respectfully and finding a shared voice.
