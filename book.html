Engagements

What Manmohan Can Bring to Your Stage

Solo Recital

Traditional Tabla Solo

A full classical tabla solo in Taal Teentaal (or other taals on request) — showcasing the complete repertoire of the Banaras Baaj: kaidas, relas, tukdas and chakradhars.

Duet

Tabla + Sitar / Flute / Vocal

Jugalbandi performances with sitar (long-standing partnership with David Beards), bansuri, or Hindustani vocal — accompaniment elevated to rhythmic dialogue.

Dance

Kathak Accompaniment

Live tabla accompaniment for classical Kathak recitals — drawing on a lifetime of dialogue with his sister, Kathak exponent Era Dogra.

Fusion

Kathak × Jazz · World Collaborations

Original cross-genre projects — fusion with jazz, flamenco (upcoming world tour with Brandon Walker), world-percussion and contemporary ensembles.

Education

Workshops & Masterclasses

Interactive workshops for universities, schools and festival audiences — rhythm as a universal language, with hands-on engagement.

Private

Private & Corporate Events

Weddings, cultural evenings, embassy receptions, corporate cultural programming — discreet, elegant performances of any length.
